You can get them pretty much anywhere if you know where to look. There's little corner stores that has stamps. You can get even get them online. I like to get a bunch of my stamps all at once. This way I won't have to worry about getting more stamps later. You can get a coil of 100 stamps for 53 cents per stamp (or 53 dollars) on Amazon. Check it out here.

I also have these pretty bird looking stamps. It's 11 dollars for 20 stamps or about 58 cents a piece. If you don't need that many then this one's pretty good. A bit more expensive though. You can check that out here.

Here's the problem with buying stamps from anywhere but a post office. The stores buy from the Post Office and charge an extra markup. That's why it's recommended to actually go to the post office or buy them online. You'll save more money that way. It won't matter if you're buying just one though. If you're sending a lot of mail then you might as well buy a bunch of them in one shot.
